Reflection of a creepy girl - Kalathigiri falls..

I heard the rushing water,
I felt the soft breeze blowing my tresses out behind me,
I saw the water falling in slow motion,
I heard the roar of the mighty waterfall,

I felt the spray as she kissed the jagged rocks,
I saw the light cast a heavenly glow on my body,
And on the water in a pattern I cannot understand.
I heard the wind whistling in my ears,

I felt the cool water running over my bare feet,
The clouds above are colored with the vibrant paintbrush from her lips,
The strokes fired up the world around me.

The waterfall is beautiful, stunning, majestic, breath-taking, a wonder of her weep
And quite reflection of a creepy girl.

Flying Across Her - Mullianagiri peak

Don’t dare to date with her, She will freeze you out.
I forgot my jacket just to feel her breath.
Her smile makes me feel like I have shared a life or two
And her eyes are always wandering over the horizon.

Her imperfections, mappings made me sat on her chest.
She’ll map her way with the coastline and follow the weather as it comes.
She grown with the seasons and evolving with the mountains.

She was more brave within sunset hues.
Less clumsy without shoes.
Anxious to move. Don’t make her choose.
A blanket of snow. A cliff’s learn call.

I left her alone and back to my life with the memories she gave..
Let her be.
